Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I install or update the app? - Visit the App Store on your iPhone. - Search for "Settlements" - Tap Install or Update if a new version is available.

2. The app is crashing or not responding. What should I do? - Ensure you have the latest version installed. - Restart your device and try reopening the app. - Check your internet connection. - If the issue persists, try reinstalling the app.
