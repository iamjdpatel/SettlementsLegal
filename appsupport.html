Settlements App Support

Welcome to Settlements. If you need assistance, you’ve come to the right place. Below you’ll find troubleshooting tips, FAQs, and contact options to get the help you need.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I install or update the app?

- Visit the App Store on your iPhone.

- Search for "Settlements"

- Tap Install or Update if a new version is available.

2. The app is crashing or not responding. What should I do?

- Ensure you have the latest version installed.

- Restart your device and try reopening the app.

- Check your internet connection.

- If the issue persists, try reinstalling the app.

3. How to add new Settlement?

- Click "+ Add Settlement" from home screen to add new Settlement.

4. How to add bill to Settlement?

- Click "+" from top right corner from "Bills" tab inside newly added Settlement. - Enter bill details like name, bill date, bill amount, who paid the bill, select members who split the bill, etc and click "Add Bill"

5. How to update bill?

- Click on bill to update bill details.

6. View Final Settlement.

- Switch to "Final" tab to view Final Settlement.

7. Add/Remove member from Settlement

- Switch to "Members" tab to add new member or remove existing member from Settlement.
