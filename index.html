Welcome to Settlements. Your privacy is important to us. We may share your information with third-party service providers, such as Firebase, who assist us in operating and maintaining the app. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

You can access and update your personal information by logging into your account and editing your profile settings.

You can request the deletion of your Settlements account in app (0.9.0 and later versions) or contacting us at iamjdpatel@icloud.com. We will process your account deletion request within 30 days.
